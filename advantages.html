Advantages

Feels Real: VR having high-definition visuals, sounds and other effects gives the user a feeling of reality. It gives the user a feeling of actually being inside a completely different world.

Simulation: It is a good platform for practicing activities. Now-a-days, through VR simulation, doctors, defense students, pilots are able to practice. It gives them a close to reality experience that could benefit them in actual situations.

Safe Learning Environment: VR creates a safe learning environment for students. It helps them experience things without actually experiencing them. Before facing the real challenges, with the help of VR, they can have an idea of the challenges and can experiment on how to resolve them.

Visit places sitting at home: One can visit places around the globe and even in outer space without physically being there.
